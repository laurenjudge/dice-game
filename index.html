Rules

  • Before starting a game, you can change the winning score at the bottom of the page
  • The game has 2 players, & it's played in rounds.
  • For each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they wish. Each result gets added to their Round Score
  • But, if the player roles a 1, all their Round Score gets lost. After this, it's the next person's turn.
  • The player can choose to "Hold" which means that their Round Score gets added to their Total Score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to total the Winning Score wins the game
